About Monkeys

Monkeys are intelligent and highly adaptable primates that inhabit various parts of the world. They exhibit a wide range of behaviors, physical characteristics, and social structures.

Types of Monkeys

Chimpanzee

Chimpanzees are highly intelligent and closely related to humans. They are known for their complex social structures, tool use, and expressive facial expressions.

Orangutan

Orangutans are large, solitary apes that inhabit the forests of Southeast Asia. They are known for their distinctive red hair and impressive arboreal (tree-dwelling) abilities.

Spider Monkey

Spider monkeys are agile primates found in Central and South America. They are known for their long limbs, prehensile tails, and the ability to move swiftly through the treetops.
