About Bikes

Bikes, also known as bicycles, are a popular mode of transportation and a source of recreation. They come in various types and designs to suit different purposes and preferences.

Types of Bikes

Mountain Bike

Mountain bikes are designed for off-road cycling and feature robust frames, wide tires, and suspension systems for tackling rough terrains and trails.

Road Bike

Road bikes are built for speed and efficiency on paved roads. They have lightweight frames, narrow tires, and drop handlebars for a more aerodynamic riding position.

Cruiser Bike

Cruiser bikes are known for their vintage-inspired design and comfortable riding experience. They typically have wide handlebars, wide seats, and balloon tires.
